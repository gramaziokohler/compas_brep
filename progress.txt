## Task: Topology sub-objects (OCC) — issue 02

- `OccBrepVertex` — wraps `TopoDS_Vertex`; `.point` lazily converts via `BRep_Tool.Pnt_s`
- `OccBrepEdge` — wraps `TopoDS_Edge`; `.curve` lazily calls `_extract_edge_curve`
